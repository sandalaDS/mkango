## Design Context

### Users
Affluent leisure travelers, golf enthusiasts, and regional business leaders seeking premium stays in Lusaka with seamless booking, concierge, and lifestyle storytelling.

### Brand Personality
Refined, hospitable, and confident—balancing Zambian warmth with European editorial polish inspired by Maison Mastrorelli.

### Aesthetic Direction
Desktop-first, asymmetric editorial layout with deep golf greens, soft gold accents, custom line icons, and cinematic photography emphasizing the resort's golf heritage and serene landscape.

### Design Principles
1. Showcase golf, suites, and dining through immersive imagery with ample breathing room.
2. Keep typography elevated: high-contrast serif display + rational sans body, tight kerning, generous leading.
3. Use motion sparingly (parallax, fades, hover lifts) to reinforce luxury pacing, honoring prefers-reduced-motion.
4. Maintain tactile micro-interactions (chat bubble, booking CTA) with full focus states and 44px hit areas.
5. Build reusable tokens/components that can extend into future Convex/Payload CMS-driven experiences.
