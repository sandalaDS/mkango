# Creative Brief — M'kango Golfview Hotel

## Audience
Global luxury travelers, Lusaka-based executives, golf members, and destination wedding planners seeking a refined getaway with seamless digital touchpoints.

## Problem Statement
Current digital presence lacks immersive storytelling and premium booking cues. We need a cinematic homepage that communicates five-star credentials while showcasing golf, rooms, dining, and personalized service.

## Promise
"A sanctuary overlooking Zambia's most serene greens"—the website should feel like a concierge-guided stroll through the resort, culminating in an effortless booking prompt.

## Visual/Motion Direction
Editorial safari-luxe: tinted ivory canvases, deep golf greens, soft gold understories, custom line icons rooted in golf + hospitality, parallax hero, staggered fades, WhatsApp concierge bubble.

## Differentiators
1. Cinematic parallax hero with aerial footage styling and dual CTAs.
2. Custom iconography & stat capsules tying golf credentials with lifestyle experiences.
3. Floating WhatsApp concierge micro-app and availability calendar preview for instant planning.
4. Layered testimonials + gallery interplay evoking boutique magazines rather than template grids.

## Checkout Experience Brief
### Audience
Hotel owners evaluating Mkango's booking UX, plus affluent travelers expecting a polished, globally familiar payment flow with local rails.

### Problem
Demo must feel like a production-ready checkout despite no real gateway—any latency or missing cues erodes confidence in the upcoming Payload integration.

### Promise
"Effortless confirmation in under a minute"—the UI mirrors international luxury brands while highlighting trusted Zambian payment patterns.

### Memorable Hook
Segmented payment toggle plus a suspenseful processing state that mimics Mobile Money authorization before revealing a bespoke receipt reference.
